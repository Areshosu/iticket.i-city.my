SnoWalk is South East Asia’s first indoor winter wonderland. It is climate-controlled at below 5 degrees Celsius with 100mm of snow underfoot and provides an unforgettable Arctic experience to anyone looking to escape the tropical heat.

It features 50 thousand square feet of floor space and over 100 tonnes of ice sculptures shaped by a team of 30 artists from Harbin, China. It also features a play zone for fun activities such as snowman-making, ice-sliding and tobogganing.

Jacket is provided. Shoes are available for rent at RM 10 per pair.
