  • Waiver Required: Prior to entering SportStars, all visitors must complete a "Waiver Form."

  • Failure to complete the form will deny entry to SportStars.

4. Safety and Conduct Rules

  • Grip Socks Required: All individuals entering SportStars are required to wear grip socks that meet SportStars's safety standards.
  • Park Attendant Instructions: All visitors must comply with all rules and instructions given by SportStars park attendants.

  • Personal Belongings: Parents and guardians are responsible for safeguarding their valuables and clothing. SportStars is not liable for any lost, stolen, or damaged items.
  • Prohibited Items:
    • Outside Food and Drinks: No outside food or beverages are permitted within the park.
    • Smoking/Vaping: Smoking, vaping, and the use of electronic cigarettes are strictly prohibited within the park.
    • Prams/wheelchairs: Prams/wheelchairs are not permitted inside SportStars due to the venue's layout and structure.
    • Jewelry and Sharp Objects: Jewelry and sharp objects are not permitted within the park. Participants must not wear or carry loose items such as steel belt buckles, key chains, or hard objects in their pockets.

  • Individuals with Disabilities (OKU): Individuals with disabilities are welcome at SportStars but MUST be accompanied and supervised by an adult or guardian at all times.
